What is Medall?

Medall is an online marketplace designed to connect individuals and businesses in Canada who are looking to buy, sell, or rent second-hand medical equipment. Our platform aims to create a more convenient and affordable way for individuals to access the medical equipment they need, while also providing a platform for sellers to reach a wider audience and earn money by selling or renting their equipment.
